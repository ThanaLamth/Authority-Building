For Indonesia-based users, local exchanges still have a real structural advantage. They usually understand the payment environment better, fit domestic expectations more naturally, and create fewer surprises around cash movement.

Global exchanges still matter for product breadth and deeper liquidity, but they are not automatically the best answer for a reader whose primary goal is straightforward IDR-based crypto access.
