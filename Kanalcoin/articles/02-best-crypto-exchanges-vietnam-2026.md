## Fees, Spreads, and Hidden Friction Matter More Than Headlines

Vietnam-based users often focus too much on headline fees and too little on real execution cost. In practice, a low advertised fee matters less if:

- P2P pricing is poor
- withdrawal routes are inconvenient
- stablecoin spreads are wider than expected
- the platform encourages overtrading through product clutter

This is why the best exchange is not simply the one with the lowest stated number on a fee page. It is the one with the cleanest real workflow for your use case.
