## Competitor pattern seen in these SERPs

Four competitor groups dominate the space:

1. Finance-affiliate publishers
   - Forbes Advisor
   - Money
   - TechRadar-style list publishers
2. Crypto editorial brands
   - Coin Bureau
   - CoinGecko Research
   - CoinCodex
3. Official product pages
   - wallet, exchange, analytics, and bridge product sites
4. Power-user communities
   - dashboards, docs, GitHub pages, protocol explainers

## Main opportunity

The gap is not "another best list."

The gap is:

- clearer use-case segmentation
- more honest risk framing
- side-by-side tradeoff explanations
- better internal-link integration with how-to and strategy pages
- direct-answer formatting that works for both SEO and GEO

## Structural recommendation

Every article should include:

1. a direct answer block
2. a methodology section
3. best-by-use-case recommendations
4. a comparison table
5. a risk section
6. an FAQ

## Refresh logic

- Wallets, exchanges, bridges, and perps: every 30-45 days
- Trackers, analytics, staking, and yield pages: every 45-60 days
- Re-run titles and descriptions if CTR lags after 4-6 weeks
