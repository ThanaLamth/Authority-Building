## General media rule

- Every article should have 1 featured image.
- Every article should have 1 table rendered as a visual block.

- If a source page is blocked in normal fetch, use CloakBrowser to capture the public page cleanly before publishing.

## Alt-text rule

Alt text should describe the information value of the image, not just the design.

Good example:

- `Chart ranking the top crypto exchanges in 2026 by market presence with notes on trust and regulation`

Weak example:

- `Crypto image with logos`
