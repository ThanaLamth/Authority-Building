## Why exchange flows still matter

Exchange flow analysis matters because it helps answer:

- is BTC moving onto exchanges or off them?
- is the move broad across venues or concentrated?
- do reserve changes confirm the narrative?
- is the market seeing distribution, accumulation, or neutral routing?

That makes exchange-flow tracking especially useful during volatility spikes and ETF-driven phases.

## How to read inflows, outflows, and reserves correctly

Inflows are not automatically bearish and outflows are not automatically bullish. The key is to ask:

- is the move unusually large?
- is it repeated across several days?
- are reserves confirming the trend?
- is spot price reacting?

Without that context, exchange-flow reporting becomes narrative bait.
