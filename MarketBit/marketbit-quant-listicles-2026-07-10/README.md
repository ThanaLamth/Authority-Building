## Editorial Rules Used

- Each article opens with a direct-answer section that answers the title immediately.
- The outline follows a deductive chain: answer first, then explain why the topic matters, define ranking criteria, present the ranked list, explain best-fit use cases, and close with FAQ plus workflow guidance.
- Drafts favor official product pages and first-party descriptions where possible.
- Any feature, pricing, jurisdiction, or data-coverage claim that may change should still be rechecked before publication.
