# NFTEnex Topic Research

Date: 2026-07-10

## Objective

Build 10 durable top/list articles for an NFT and digital ownership specialist site, with a bias toward:

- category-defining comparisons
- creator-economy infrastructure
- NFT market tooling
- evergreen digital ownership explainers with commercial intent

## Chosen Clusters

1. `nft-infrastructure/minting`
2. `nft-markets/trading-data`
3. `creator-economy/royalties`
4. `nft-infrastructure/storage`
5. `nft-culture/communities`
6. `nft-infrastructure/metadata`
7. `creator-economy/artists`
8. `nft-markets/marketplaces`
9. `gaming-nft/games`

## Why These 10 Topics

- Marketplace and game roundups still have the broadest ceiling, but they are more competitive.
- Royalties, storage, metadata, minting, analytics, tracking, and APIs are stronger authority-builders because they force clearer explanations and more useful comparison frameworks.
- Artist and creator-focused comparisons fit the site's digital-ownership angle better than generic hype coverage.
- Community and gaming pages help cover the culture and utility layer of NFT ownership, not just trading.

## Current Research Signals

These were reviewed on 2026-07-10:

- Alchemy currently surfaces a live ecosystem page for NFT minting tools and another for NFT analytics tools.
- Zerion has a 2026 NFT API guide.
- DappRadar maintains marketplace and game coverage that is useful for shortlist validation.
- OpenSea and Magic Eden both maintain current help-center documentation for fees and creator-facing marketplace behavior.
- OpenSea now documents the metadata field on NFT pages, which supports a stronger storage/metadata article angle.
- IPFS documentation still provides durable best practices for storing NFT data.
- Hive Index maintains a dedicated NFT communities directory, which helps validate that the topic is still active and segmentable.

## Drafting Standard

Every article in this batch should:

- answer the query high on the page
- include a quick-pick or comparison block
- define evaluation criteria before deeper reviews
- separate best-for-use-case recommendations from overall rankings
- avoid fake precision where 2026 details change quickly
- leave `[needs source]` markers for final product, fee, volume, or policy checks

## Packaging Decision

All 10 drafts are grouped under `nftenex/articles/` so they can move into sourcing and publication as one topical batch.
