- `/trends/`

## Notes

- The first H2 in every article answers the title directly.
- The remaining sections follow a chained explanatory structure: direct answer, methodology, ranked list, evidence, meaning, FAQ.
- These drafts are written to be publishable after final fact-checking. Where a live number, quote, or legal interpretation still needs tighter sourcing, the article keeps a brief editor checklist rather than pretending certainty.
